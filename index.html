Video Object Tracking

As our final project in the course Computer Vision, I implemented the mean shift tracking algorithm. This tracker is histogram-based and utilizes the statistics of a region, which makes it well suited for tracking deformable objects. One runs the MATLAB program on a video, sets the tracker to the coordinates of an object, and the program will redraw the bounding box for the object at every frame.
